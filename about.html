About SteamTeen

What is STEAMTEEN?

Welcome to STEAMTEEN, where curiousity is encouraged and creativity blossoms! STEAMTEEN is a national conference dedicated to exposing teenagers to STEAM fields. We primarily cater to teenagers ages 13-18, but anyone is welcome to register and attend a conference! We accept everyone regardless of their background and strive to create a safe and welcoming community for all. While we do accept donations, our conferences are 100% free for ALL participants. The only things that are required in order to be fully immersed in the events are internet connection, access to Zoom, and a passion for learning! This year, our 2022 virtual conference will be held on Zoom from July 6th to July 8th. Visit our sign up page or click here to register!
