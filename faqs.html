Frequently Asked Questions

When does registration for STEAMTEEN 2022 close?

Registration for the National STEAMTEEN Conference 2022 closes Tuesday, May 31, 2022. Make sure that you sign up before then to claim your spot!

What are the dates for the National STEAMTEEN Conference 2023?

The dates for the National STEAMTEEN Conference 2023 will be posted in December 2022 and registration will open on January 1, 2023.

How do I know if I am elligible to register and attend a STEAMTEEN conference?

While STEAMTEEN primarily caters to teenagers ages 13-18, anyone is welcome to register and attend a conference! We accept everyone regardless of their background and promote a safe environment for all. The only things that are required in order to attain a full STEAMTEEN experience are internet connection, access to Zoom, and a passion for learning!

I already attended a STEAMTEEN conference in the past, can I still attend another conference?

Absolutely! There is no limit to how many times you can attend a STEAMTEEN conference. While we do stay true to our STEAM values, our guest speakers and activities vary by year so you will be guaranteed a new and interesting experience every time!

Will STEAMTEEN 2023 take place virtually or in-person?

Unfortunately, we do not know yet whether the 2023 STEAMTEEN conference will be held virtually or in-person as it depends on the status of COVID-19 at that time. We will annouce all information regarding STEAMTEEN 2023 sometime in December of 2022.
