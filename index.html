About

STEAMTEEN is a national conference that encourages teenagers to develop interests in the world of STEAM (science, technology, engineering, art, and mathematics) and the careers the future holds! STEAMTEEN especially focuses on appealing to teenagers ages 13-18, but anyone may attend a conference! Our conferences are 100% free for all participants. This year, our 2022 virtual conference will be held on Zoom from July 6th to July 8th. You can click this link to register or visit our about page to learn more!
