STEAM TRACKS

Science

Science is how people study the structural and behavioral aspects of the world by observing and experimenting. It studies both physical and natural occurances while providing answers to questions about the unknown.

View
Technology

Technology is the application of scientific knowledge for useful situations. Many technological situations usually include special equipment that performs a specific job for the user.

View
Engineering

Engineering is a discipline that combines science, technology, art, and math in order to design, build, and innovate products and structures for the use of the public.

View
Art

Art is the expression of creativity and imagination. It is mostly displayed in a visual form like a drawing or model and can be portrayed as a provocative, emotional, and/or attractive figure.

View
Mathematics

Mathematics, or math, is the analysis and application of numbers, quantities, and positive or negative space. Math can be classified as pure or applied mathematics depending on the circumstances in which it is utilized.
